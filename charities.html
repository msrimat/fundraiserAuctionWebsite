Co-Caring Initiative & the Female and Free Speech Airlift

We are a coalition of high-powered volunteers affiliated with science institutes, top consulting firms, media protection organizations, advocacy groups, public health organizations, national security groups and some bad ass Navy vets â€” from Silicon Valley to Washington DC.

We collected a list of hundreds of high-risk Afghans, have helped over 400 people evacuate and continue to focus on the most vulnerable, including outspoken women, creatives and scholars (scientists, filmmakers, journalists, activists) and their families who largely don't have US visas.

Our coalition is working out the logistics to get these Afghans to the Kabul airport and through the gates; hire and land a charter plane; get them on the plane; arrive at a country that will accept them for transfer, and also get them visas to other countries. Each of these steps is incredibly complicated.

But we are up to the task.
